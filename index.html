Our Story

Founded by Hemanshu Gupta, a passionate entrepreneur with a vision to bring pure, organic dairy products to every household in Dehri-on-Sone and beyond.

At Sri Krishna Dairy, we believe in the ancient wisdom of Ayurveda and traditional farming. Our products come from healthy, happy cows of indigenous breeds, raised without chemicals or hormones.

Every product is a testament to our commitment to quality, purity, and your family's health.
